# Customer Churn Risk Analysis

## Business Problem
A telecom company is losing 26.5% of customers 
annually — $1.67M in revenue at risk.

## Key Findings
- Month-to-month contracts = 42.7% churn rate
- No tech support = 41.6% churn rate
- 2,735 high-risk customers identified (50% churn)
- Built risk scoring system (0-100) for every customer

## Tools
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn

## Results
- Segmented 7,043 customers into 3 risk categories
- High risk customers churn at 8x rate of low risk
- 3 retention strategies with $500K+ revenue impact
